Know About iMeUsWe

Our Mission

We believe in Bonding Families together by reviving memories through shared family stories. Such stories help in reliving the joy of togetherness and build a yearning to experience it often, thereby creating a sense of belonging and pride, apart from preserving our heritage and unity.

We live in times of great mobility. Families drift apart and lose touch. Family members feel disconnected and isolated. Children and grandchildren are no longer in touch with their extended family. Family information and history are scattered and not easily available. The Indian diaspora – over 30 million and the largest for any country – is at risk of being alienated from Indian culture and heritage.

With a growing loss of a sense of belonging, future generations could end up feeling rootless and insecure. iMeUsWe brings families together and provides a platform to discover, connect, share and preserve their heritage for posterity.

The iMeUsWe Advantage
